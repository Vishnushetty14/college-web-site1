The department of EECE is a constituent department in GITAM Institute of Technology, GITAM (Deemed to be University). The vision of the department is to excel in education by imparting quality teaching and research to meet the challenges in Electrical, Electronics and Communication Engineering. The department has highly competent faculty with high retention and varied specializations. It is well equipped with State-of-Art Laboratories. Students experience holistic learning with the aid of Personality Development /Soft Skills training, Mini Projects, Design Practice, Foreign Languages, Industrial Training & Internship, Professional Ethics & Human Values. Students pass out from this department with good placements and progression statistics. The department is offering two undergraduate B.Tech programs in ECE, EEE and five post graduate M.Tech programs with specialization in VLSI Design, Radio Frequency & Microwave engineering, Embedded Systems, Power systems & Automation and Electronics Design & Technology (with CDAC, Hyderabad). The department of EECE look forward in welcoming potential learners to the energetic and electrifying learning environment.
